Philippine Heroes

Philippine heroes are the brave men and women who dedicated their lives to fight for our country’s freedom, dignity, and identity. Through courage, sacrifice, and love for the nation, they stood up against colonizers, injustice, and oppression. From national figures like Dr. José Rizal and Andrés Bonifacio to unsung heroes in every region, their stories remind us that patriotism comes in many forms — through words, actions, and even silent resistance. Their legacy continues to inspire generations of Filipinos to uphold the values of freedom, unity, and resilience.

Dr. José Rizal

Dr. José Rizal is a national hero of the Philippines, known for his role in the fight for independence through his writings.

Andrés Bonifacio

Andrés Bonifacio was the founder of the Katipunan and a key figure in the Philippine Revolution.

Corazon Aquino

Corazon Aquino was the first female President of the Philippines and a symbol of democracy.

Lapu-Lapu

The OG hero. He defeated Magellan in the Battle of Mactan in 1521. Hindi kami pasisiil!

Gabriela Silang

First Filipina to lead a revolt against the Spanish. A fearless warrior and icon of women empowerment.

Anotonio Luna

A brilliant general with a short temper but big heart for the nation. Known for his discipline and strategy.

Melchora Aquino

The “Mother of the Katipunan.” She gave shelter and care to revolutionaries. Hero grandma realness!

Apolinario Mabini

Known as the "Brains of the Revolution." Even though he was paralyzed, his mind was sharper than ever.
