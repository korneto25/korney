The Philippines is a beautiful archipelago located in Southeast Asia, known for its rich culture, stunning beaches, and warm, friendly people. It is made up of over 7,000 islands and is divided into three main geographical regions:

Philippine Heroes

Dr. José Rizal

Dr. José Rizal is a national hero of the Philippines, known for his pivotal role in the country's fight for independence through his writings.

Andrés Bonifacio

Andrés Bonifacio was a revolutionary leader and founder of the Katipunan, an organization that fought for Philippine independence.

Corazon Aquino

Corazon Aquino was the first female President of the Philippines and a symbol of democracy and freedom during the People Power Revolution.
