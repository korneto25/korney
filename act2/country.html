MABUHAY! WELCOME TO THE PHILIPPINES

The Philippines is a beautiful archipelago located in Southeast Asia, known for its rich culture, stunning beaches, and warm, friendly people. It is made up of over 7,000 islands and is divided into three main geographical regions: Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao.

Marvel at unique geological formations in Bohol.

Visayas

Relax on white sandy beaches of this world-renowned island.

Mindanao

Admire the perfect cone shape of this active volcano in Albay.

Must-Try Foods

Enjoy local delicacies like Adobo, Sinigang, and Lechon.

Famous Personalities

Celebrate achievements of Manny Pacquiao, Jose Rizal, and Lea Salonga.
